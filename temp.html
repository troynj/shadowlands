About Shadowlands

Shadowlands is a new and exciting adventure game inspired by Pokemon. Explore the world of Shadows and capture, train, and battle these mysterious creatures. With a variety of diverse regions to explore, you'll never run out of challenges to face.

Features

  • Capturable Shadows
  • Diverse Regions
  • Train Your Shadows
  • Battle System
  • Online Tournaments
